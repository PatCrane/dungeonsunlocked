Dungeons Unlocked: Tabletop Role-Playing Games for the Rest of Us

Dungeons Unlocked is a free set of table-top style RPG rules. The aim is to provide an accessible set of free resources to any aspiring group of gamers interested in playing fantasy role-playing games. The game rules are designed to be easy to understand to allow for seamless and intuitive gameplay.

Requirements

  • Something to write or type on.
  • At least one D6 (six-sided dice)

What DungeonsUnlocked does NOT require:

  • Costumes/LARPing: If you are just getting into table-top gaming, you may imagine a group of folks wearing wizard robes, brandishing foam swords and hurling tennis balls as magical projectiles. This is not at all required to play the game. However, you can definitely incorporate costumes and LARPing if you are so inclined.
